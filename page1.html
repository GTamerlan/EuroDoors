Operations, Product & Supply

Business Model

Dual channel revenue: B2B (contractors, designers, showrooms) plus B2C (direct showroom and inbound leads). Repeat and referral business is growing.

Product Lines

  • Flush doors — minimal, modern panels
  • Frameless doors — hidden frames for seamless walls
  • Modular panel doors — European-style component system for rapid customization

Modular Frame-Panel System

Stock stiles, rails and inserts are combinable, enabling 3–4× more model permutations from the same inventory. This reduces carrying cost and accelerates delivery for custom sizing.
