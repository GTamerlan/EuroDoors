Executive Summary

Founded to address the growing demand for modern, European-style interior doors in the U.S. market, our company is a Southern California-based manufacturer and distributor of premium interior doors. With a strong emphasis on quality, customization, and operational efficiency, we’ve built a reputation for delivering flush, frameless, and modular luxury door systems that appeal to both contractors and end consumers.

The company is now being offered for sale as the owner seeks a strategic partner or acquirer to accelerate growth. This presents a rare opportunity to acquire a turnkey operation with a scalable business model and strong early momentum in a booming sector.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To redefine interior architecture by delivering premium, customizable, and design-forward door solutions that enhance modern living spaces.

Vision: To become the leading luxury interior door brand in North America, known for custom fitment, European aesthetics, and fast delivery.

Industry Position

We serve the mid-to-high-end residential and light commercial construction market, offering products that fill a clear gap between commodity-grade doors and ultra-premium imports. Competitors include traditional door suppliers (e.g., Home Depot, Lowe’s) and boutique European importers — yet few combine custom sizing, local assembly, component-based flexibility, and premium finishes as we do.

Business Model

The company generates revenue through the sale of interior door sets, both pre-configured and fully customized, through two primary sales channels:

  • · B2B: Contractors, designers, developers, and showroom partners
  • · B2C: Homeowners via showroom visits and direct inquiries

Product Range

  • · Flush doors with clean, modern lines for contemporary spaces
  • · Frameless doors that offer a sleek, minimal aesthetic with hidden frames
  • · Modular panel doors (European construction) that enable scalable customization and inventory efficiency

Modular Frame-Panel System

A major innovation lies in our modular frame-panel system, which allows stock components (vertical stiles, horizontal rails, and inserts) to be mixed and matched across models. This enables us to:

  • · Build multiple door styles from shared core inventory
  • · Offer rapid customization of size, finish, and inserts (glass, wood, etc.)
  • · Produce 3–4x more model variations from the same materials vs. traditional pre-assembled door inventory

This flexibility reduces lead times, expands design options, and helps us meet the aesthetic and budgetary needs of a wide range of customers — from value-conscious homeowners to luxury custom builders.

Customer Base

We serve a growing network of contractors, architects, and designers, as well as direct homeowners. Most projects involve custom homes, remodels, and multi-unit residential developments, with a growing share of repeat and referral business.

Supply Chain

We import proprietary modular components from vetted suppliers in Europe and China, then size and assemble each door in California. This hybrid model offers:

  • - Maximum design flexibility
  • - Inventory cost control
  • - Faster fulfillment times
  • - Customization without full retooling

Employees

  • - Small, lean team including sales, assembly, and logistics
  • - Owner-led operations
  • - Easily scalable with increased demand

Facilities & Equipment

  • - Modern showroom in Los Angeles designed to reflect the brand’s luxury identity
  • - In-house assembly workshop for precise sizing, machining, and packaging

Core equipment includes:

  • - Advanced CNC machine, calibrated for:
  •     • Multiple door types: Swing, Pocket, Bypass, Magic sliding, Double doors

NEW
TECHNOLOGIES

Engineering • Automation • Rapid Customization

Engineered Euro-Block Core

Multi-layer, laminated solid-wood core faced with high-density MDF and real wood veneer for warp resistance, screw-holding strength, and long-term dimensional stability. Delivers solid feel, superior sound dampening, and stable anchoring for concealed hinges & premium hardware.

Modular Frame-Panel System

Interchangeable stiles, rails, and inserts (glass/wood/metal) enable rapid style creation from shared inventory, producing 3–4× more model variations from the same stock, with faster lead times and lower carrying costs.

CNC Precision & CAD-to-Shop

Programmed toolpaths for swing, pocket, bypass, and double-door sets; precise pre-cuts for European handles, American locksets, radius & concealed hinges. Enables repeatable tolerance control, faster changeovers, and cleaner fitment that reduces install time & rework.

Automated Finishing Line

Closed spray environment with multi-stage sanding/polishing for consistent, dust-free coatings and premium sheen. Durable, chemical-resistant finishes with uniform color-matching across large orders; factory pre-finish lowers total installed cost.

Complete Door Systems

Pre-finished slabs + matching frames/jambs, trims, seals, and premium hardware ship as a kit for rapid installation. Fewer site trades, predictable timelines, consistent results, and high customer satisfaction & referrals.

Performance & Scale

Solid-core mass + integrated seals improve acoustic comfort; tall formats (8–9 ft) and non-standard widths available on short lead times. Local assembly shortens schedules with in-house quality control, scaling across SKUs without quality loss.
